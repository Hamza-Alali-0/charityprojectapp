Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

How can I start a fundraising campaign?

To start a fundraising campaign, you need to register as an organization. Once approved, you can create and manage campaigns through your organization dashboard.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. All transactions are secure and encrypted to protect your personal information.

How do I know my donation reaches the intended recipients?

We have a strict verification process for all organizations, and each campaign includes detailed reporting. You can track the impact of your donation through campaign updates.

Can I volunteer instead of making a financial contribution?

Absolutely! Many campaigns welcome volunteers. Check the campaign details for volunteer opportunities or contact the organization directly.
